BINI is a 8-member Filipino girl group under Star Magic of ABS-CBN. The group consists of Jhoanna, Aiah, Colet, Maloi, Gwen, Stacey, Mikha, and Sheena. They released their pre-debut single, “Da Coconut Nut,” on November 6th, 2020. BINI officially debuted on June 11th, 2021 with another single, “Born To Win.”
